Introduction to Japanese Tea

Japan has a variety of tea types. Here, we introduce some of the most representative ones.

Green Tea

Green tea is the most common tea in Japan. It is highly nutritious and considered good for health, especially rich in vitamin C and catechins.

Matcha

Matcha is powdered green tea used in tea ceremonies. It is known for its rich flavor and vibrant green color.

Hojicha

Hojicha is roasted green tea with a distinctive toasty aroma. It has low caffeine content, making it a good choice for drinking in the evening.

Genmaicha

Genmaicha is a blend of green tea and roasted brown rice. It has a unique aroma and flavor.
